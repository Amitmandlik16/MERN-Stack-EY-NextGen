PuranPoli


Puran poli is a signature Maharashtrian dish

Puran Poli is a popular sweet flatbread from Maharashtra, India. It is a delectable combination of a sweet lentil filling called “puran” encased in a soft and thin wheat flour dough. This traditional delicacy holds a special place in Maharashtrian cuisine and is often prepared during festivals, family gatherings, and auspicious occasions.
